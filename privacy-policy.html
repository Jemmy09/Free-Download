Privacy Policy

How we handle your information when you use FreeDownload

Last updated: January 1, 2026

← Back to Home

👋 Overview

Welcome to FreeDownload. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

FreeDownload is a free software discovery and download platform. No account registration is required to browse or download software. The only login system on this platform is restricted to site administrators.

📋 Information We Collect

Because no user registration is required, we collect minimal information:

  • Browser storage (localStorage): We store your software library preferences locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated data such as page views and download button clicks to improve the platform. This data contains no personally identifiable information.
  • IP address: Your IP address may be logged by our hosting provider as part of standard server access logs. We do not use this to identify you personally.

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal details
  • We do not require you to create an account
  • We do not track your downloads or browsing history across sessions
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics cookies

⬇ Downloads

When you click a download button, you are redirected directly to the official website or official distribution server of the software publisher. FreeDownload does not host, modify, or repackage any software files.

We are not responsible for the content, safety, or licensing of software obtained from third-party sources. Always verify downloads from official publishers.

🍪 Cookies

FreeDownload does not use tracking cookies. We use browser localStorage solely to remember your software library state between visits. This is stored entirely on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

🔐 Admin Access

The only login functionality on this platform is the administrator panel, which is not publicly accessible or linked. Admin credentials are managed privately and are not associated with any public user accounts.

Admin session data is stored in sessionStorage and is automatically cleared when the browser tab is closed.

🔗 Third-Party Links

Our platform contains links to third-party websites and download servers. Once you leave FreeDownload, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

🛡️ Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data stored on our servers to be breached. All software library data is stored locally in your own browser. We implement standard web security practices including HTTPS to protect data in transit.

👶 Children's Privacy

FreeDownload does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our platform does not require registration and does not collect personal information from any users, including minors.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

📬 Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through our GitHub repository at github.com/Jemmy09/Free-Download.
